# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## 我们的承诺 / Our Pledge

为了营造一个开放友好的社区环境，我们作为贡献者和维护者承诺：无论年龄、体型、可见或不可见的残障、种族、性别认同与表达、经验水平、教育程度、社会经济地位、国籍、外貌、人种、宗教信仰，或性取向，参与本项目和社区的每个人都将获得无骚扰的体验。

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## 我们的标准 / Our Standards

有助于营造积极环境的行为示例 / Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- 使用友善和包容的语言 / Using welcoming and inclusive language
- 尊重不同的观点和经历 / Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- 优雅地接受建设性批评 / Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- 关注社区的最大利益 / Focusing on what is best for the community
- 对其他社区成员表示同理心 / Showing empathy towards other community members

不可接受的行为示例 / Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- 使用带有性意味的语言或图像，以及任何形式的性挑逗 / The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances of any kind
- 挑衅、侮辱或贬损性评论，以及人身或政治攻击 / Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- 公开或私下的骚扰 / Public or private harassment
- 未经明确许可，发布他人的隐私信息（如住址、邮箱等） / Publishing others' private information without their explicit permission
- 其他可被合理认定为不专业或不当的行为 / Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## 执行责任 / Enforcement Responsibilities

项目维护者负责澄清和执行可接受行为的标准，并对任何被认为不当、威胁、冒犯或有害的行为采取适当且公平的纠正措施。

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## 适用范围 / Scope

本行为准则适用于所有项目空间（Issues、Pull Requests、Discussions、邮件等），以及在公共空间代表本项目或社区时的个人行为。

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (Issues, Pull Requests, Discussions, email, etc.) and also applies when an individual is representing the project or its community in public spaces.

## 执行 / Enforcement

可通过 <support@sxfroute.com> 向项目维护者举报辱骂、骚扰或其他不可接受的行为。所有投诉都将被审查和调查，并将以认为必要和适当的方式作出回应。维护者有义务为事件举报者保密。

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project team at <support@sxfroute.com>. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances. The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.
